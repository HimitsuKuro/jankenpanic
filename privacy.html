Privacy Policy

Jan Ken Panic — Himitsu Labs  |  Last updated: July 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Himitsu Labs ("we", "us") handles information in connection with the mobile game Jan Ken Panic ("the Game").

1. Data We Collect Directly

The Game does not require an account and does not collect your name, email, or any personally identifiable information. Your scores and settings are stored locally on your device only and are never sent to our servers.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The Game displays ads through Google AdMob. To serve ads, Google's SDK may automatically collect and process certain technical data, including:

  • the device advertising identifier (Advertising ID);
  • the IP address and device information (model, OS, language);
  • ad interaction data.

This data is used to serve ads, measure their performance, and prevent fraud, and is processed by Google as a third party. Learn more:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • How Google uses data (partners)

3. Your Choices

You can reset or delete your advertising identifier and limit ad personalization at any time from your device settings (Android: Settings → Google → Ads).

4. Consent (EEA, UK, Switzerland)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the Game shows a consent form (Google's User Messaging Platform) the first time you play, asking whether you agree to personalized ads. You can change or withdraw your choice at any time from the in-game Options menu → "Privacy Options".

5. Children

The Game is intended for a general audience and is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country).

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location (for example under the GDPR), you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict its processing. As advertising-related data is processed by Google, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy to exercise these rights regarding advertising data.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

8. Contact

For any question about this Privacy Policy, contact us at himitsulabs.contact@gmail.com.
